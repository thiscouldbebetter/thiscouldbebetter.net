Blog

This Could Be Better started out as a WordPress.com blog concerned mostly with brain-dead programming tutorials. The archives of that blog is still available at WordPress.com, though it hasn't seen many updates in recent years, not least because the user interface on WordPress.com gets a little harder to use every year. The current plan is to export that blog and host it on this site, where it is to be hoped, at the very least, that updating a post won't result in half of it disappearing and the rest being reformatted willy-nilly as if by whimsical forest sprites.

In the meantime, please visit https://thiscouldbebetter.wordpress.com .
